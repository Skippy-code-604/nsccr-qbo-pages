NSCCR QuickBooks Connector

A private, self-hosted integration for NSCCR Renovations Ltd, a renovation business in North Vancouver, BC.

This is a small, single-company application that connects to one QuickBooks Online company — the owner's own books — to help review invoices, accounts-receivable aging, and customer records, and (when explicitly enabled) to record payments and create invoices.

It is not a public product. It is operated by and for NSCCR Renovations Ltd on the owner's own computer. No QuickBooks data is shared with, sold to, or transmitted to any third party; the only external service it communicates with is Intuit's QuickBooks Online API.
