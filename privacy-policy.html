Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the NSCCR QuickBooks Connector ("the App") handles data. The App is a private, self-hosted tool operated by NSCCR ("we", "us") for its own internal bookkeeping. It is not offered to, or used by, the public.

1. Who operates the App

The App is operated solely by NSCCR, North Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Contact: shane@nsccr.ca.

2. What data the App accesses

When connected to our QuickBooks Online company, the App may read and (only when the operator explicitly enables write access) modify accounting data via Intuit's QuickBooks Online API, including: company information, customers, invoices, payments, and accounts-receivable reports. The App accesses only the single QuickBooks Online company that the operator authorizes — our own.

3. How data is used

Data is used exclusively to help the operator review and manage NSCCR's own books (for example, listing invoices, checking who owes money, and recording payments). The App performs no analytics, advertising, profiling, or any processing of other parties' data.

4. Where data is stored

The App runs on the operator's own computer. OAuth credentials (client ID/secret and refresh/access tokens) are stored locally in configuration files (.env and tokens.json) on that machine. QuickBooks records are fetched on demand and are not retained in any separate database; any copies exist only transiently in memory or in the operator's own working files.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose QuickBooks data to any third party. The only external service the App communicates with is Intuit's QuickBooks Online API, over an encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connection, in order to provide the App's functionality.

6. Data retention & deletion

Stored credentials remain on the operator's machine until deleted. The operator can revoke access at any time by disconnecting the App from within QuickBooks Online and deleting the local .env and tokens.json files. See the Disconnect page.

7. Security

Credentials are kept in local files that are excluded from version control and not transmitted anywhere except to Intuit for authentication. Access tokens are short-lived and refreshed automatically; refresh tokens rotate on use. The operator is responsible for the physical and account security of the machine the App runs on.

8. Children's privacy

The App is a business bookkeeping tool and is not directed to children.

9. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy: shane@nsccr.ca.
