Connect / Reconnect

The NSCCR QuickBooks Connector is a private, self-hosted application operated by NSCCR Renovations Ltd. It connects to a single authorized QuickBooks Online company through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow. There is no hosted web app to sign in to — connecting and reconnecting are done by the operator on their own machine.

Connect for the first time

  1. Authorize the app against the QuickBooks Online company using Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow (scope com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting).
  2. Store the resulting credentials locally in the app's .env / tokens.json.
  3. Start the connector with npm start.

Reconnect

If access expires or was revoked, repeat the authorization above to obtain a fresh refresh token, update the local credentials, and restart the connector. The app refreshes short-lived access tokens automatically and rotates the refresh token on use, so reconnection is only needed after a long idle period or an explicit disconnect.

To revoke access, see the Disconnect page.
